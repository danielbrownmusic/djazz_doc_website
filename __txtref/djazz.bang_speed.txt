NAME

When active, will output beats in the polyrhythm given by numerator/denominator. Incoming beats will not be output, except for those that fall in phase with the output beats. 

INLETS
0 bang
1 tempo
2 messages: active, numerator, denominator

OUTLETS
0 bangs over time in the polyrhythm determined by numerator/denominator
1 tempo using output bangs as beat
